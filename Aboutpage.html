About Gentrification:

Gentrification is defined as "The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it comforms to middle-class taste"(Google). This may seem like a good thing, something to improve the neighborhood but it could displace or distroy families and small businesses.

Signs Of Gentrification:
  • There is construction in richmond happening
  • Local businesses are being closed
  • Public transportation is better
  • More urban art
  • Houses and sidewalks are better
