About Gentrification:

Gentrification is defined as "The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it comforts to middle-class taste"(Google). This may seem like a good thing, something to improve the neighborhood but it could displace or destroy families and small businesses.

Signs Of Gentrification:
  • There is construction in richmond happening
  • Local businesses are being closed
  • Public transportation is better
  • More urban
  • Houses and sidewalks look new
Type Of People It Affects, And Why It's Dangerous:

The type of people that gentrification affects are average people, usually middle to low class. As well as small local business owners. Many people get displaced and usually lose their homes. It's a big problem today because it could destroy local business. And make the house prices go higher than usual, making people move to a less expensive place, and this happens to low income and usually people of color.
